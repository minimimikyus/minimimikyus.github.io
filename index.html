About Me

Concept Artist

Colbi is an Illustration/Animation BFA student at the University of Connecticut from Enfield, CT. Specializing in digital illustration, she finds joy in creating work that makes people want to learn how to draw. She embraces the process of ideation and creating, often combining the real world with pop culture in her visual storytelling. She expands her skills of thumbnailing, character design and implementing feedback as she continues her third year of the UConn School of Fine Arts’ Illustration/Animation program.

Colbi’s currently entering her third year of weekly page designs, editorial illustrations and comics for The Daily Campus, UConn’s student-run newspaper.
